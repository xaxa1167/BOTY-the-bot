                    BOT SETUP
. Go to https://discord.com/developers/applications
. Click add application
. Give it a name
. Click bot
. Reset token
. Copy token
. Paste token into tokenfile.json
. Go to OAuth2
. Go to URL generator
. Select bot
. Select administrator
. Copy Link
. Open link in browser
. Add to your server
. Set a prefix in botconfig.json
Done! Start it with Start.bat
